What I Have Learned

With my teammates, I have learned that group projects require everyone's approval. I can't just start working; we all have to decide what is best for the project. For example, I can't just change something in a project, because someone else might not like the change. I would never have learned this by myself, because without other people around, I would just work to my liking.

Advice

To other young women skeptical about computer science:
I would advise you to give it a shot! Take a class in programming or computer science to see if you like it first. Make sure it's a short class; in case you end up not liking it (which is totally fine!), you can quickly stop. Join a group or watch youtube videos on the topic before fully submerging yourself.
